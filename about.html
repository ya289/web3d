About This Project

Bridging art and technology through immersive 3D beverage experiences

Web 3D Applications Project

The EsrCola 3D Exploration project showcases the powerful intersection of web technology and 3D visualization. Created for the Web 3D Applications module, this interactive platform reimagines how users can engage with beverage products in a digital space.

Our mission was to transform static product displays into dynamic, explorable 3D experiences that engage users and demonstrate the potential of web-based 3D rendering. Each model has been meticulously crafted to capture the essence and details of the original product.

The project utilizes X3DOM technology to seamlessly integrate complex 3D models directly into the browser without plugins, providing an accessible experience across modern devices and platforms.

Our collection features three distinct beverage containers, each with unique modeling challenges:

  • CokeCan.x3d - A precision-modeled aluminum can featuring authentic texturing, reflective properties, and brand-accurate details
  • SpriteBottle.x3d - A transparent glass model with complex refraction effects, realistic liquid simulation, and intricate cap threading
  • DrPepperBottle.x3d - A sculptural plastic bottle with accurate material properties, detailed label rendering, and authentic contours

Technology Stack

  • HTML5 & CSS3 for semantic structure and advanced styling
  • JavaScript ES6+ for dynamic interactions and model manipulation
  • Bootstrap 4.6 for responsive grid layout and UI components
  • X3DOM framework for WebGL-based 3D rendering without plugins
  • jQuery for streamlined DOM manipulation and event handling
  • FancyBox for immersive image gallery experiences
  • AOS for scroll-triggered animations and visual engagement
  • Blender 2.80 for professional-grade 3D modeling and texturing

Learning Achievements

Through this project, we've demonstrated mastery of:

  • Advanced 3D modeling techniques with professional workflows
  • WebGL-based 3D rendering for cross-platform compatibility
  • Modern UI/UX design principles for intuitive user interaction
  • Responsive design techniques ensuring mobile-to-desktop functionality
  • Performance optimization for complex 3D content delivery

Development Process

Our development journey began with extensive research into beverage container design, followed by detailed sketching and prototyping in Blender. Each model underwent multiple iterations to achieve photorealistic quality while maintaining web performance.

The development workflow incorporated agile methodologies, with continuous testing across browsers and devices to ensure compatibility. Special attention was given to optimizing polygon counts and texture resolutions to balance visual quality with loading speed.

Design Approach

Our design philosophy embraces the vibrant heritage of iconic beverage brands while introducing modern UI elements. The color palette draws inspiration from the products themselves, creating a cohesive visual language throughout the experience.

We've implemented a mobile-first approach with intuitive touch controls for 3D manipulation. Micro-interactions and subtle animations provide feedback and delight, guiding users naturally through the exploration of each model's features and details.

Future Improvements

Our roadmap includes implementing advanced lighting simulation with global illumination, allowing users to explore products in different environmental contexts. We're also exploring real-time fluid dynamics to simulate pouring and carbonation effects.

Future releases will incorporate WebXR capabilities, enabling users to place virtual products in their physical space through augmented reality. We're also developing collaborative viewing features allowing multiple users to explore and discuss models simultaneously.
